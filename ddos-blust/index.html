How to play

  • Click a piece, then click a free spot on the board.
  • Pieces cannot overlap or go outside the grid.
  • Fill an entire row or column to clear it and earn points.
  • After you place all 3 pieces, a new set appears.
  • If no piece fits anywhere, the game is over.
